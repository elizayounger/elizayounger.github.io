    About Me

    Background


    I grew up in Scotland on the isle of Islay and moved to the mainland at the age of 10. I’m based in Perth, but looking to relocate to a bigger city. I currently work part-time as an accounts manager for Horizon Management.

    Music


    I attended the Junior Conservatoire of Scotland for piano, harp and singing, where I achieved my grade 8 with Distinction in all 3 instruments.

    I went on to study a Music MA at the University of Glasgow. I graduated from university with the intention of becoming a full-time musician, gigging and performing at events, functions, weddings, funerals etc

    Additionally, Check out some of my performances with my band ‘Red Skye’.

    Changing Career


    After COVID, I began to take on work as a musician, pursuing a full-time career. I found that as a solo performer with bookings predominantly in evenings or weekends, my schedule was very at odds with family and friends, making me very isolated. I began to consider other career avenues and was drawn to programming through my interest in studio work and recording software. I loved the creativity that software engineering could facilitate, and the fulfilment from building complete projects.

    My Journey in Tech


    I have recently completed both Harvard’s CS50 and Code First Girls Software Engineering CFGDegree courses, achieving a 94% Distinction in the latter. These courses were undertaken alongside my work and personal commitments, showing a strong work ethic and dedication to a new career in tech.
